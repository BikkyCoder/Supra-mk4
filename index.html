The Supra MK4 twin turbo system is one of the most sophisticated forced induction designs ever implemented.

How Twin-Turbo Works:

  • Low RPM (0-4,000): Only CT12A primary turbo spools
  • Smooth Acceleration: Gradual boost ramp to avoid sudden pressure
  • High RPM (4,000+): CT12B secondary turbo activates
  • Dual Flow: Both turbos work simultaneously at high RPM
  • Linear Power Delivery: No step or lag between turbos

Technical Specifications:

  • Primary Turbo (CT12A): Garrett model
  • Secondary Turbo (CT12B): Garrett model
  • Boost Pressure Stock: 11 PSI (0.75 bar)
  • Max Safe Boost: 20+ PSI (1.4 bar)
  • Switchover Point: ~4,000 RPM

‚ö° Advantages of Twin-Turbo

  • Low-end Torque: Excellent from 3,000 RPM
  • No Turbo Lag: Smooth power band (no step)
  • Spool-up Time: ~2-3 seconds to full boost

  • Highway Driving: Excellent driveability

  • Smaller turbos = faster response
