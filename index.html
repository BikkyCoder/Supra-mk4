The Legendary 2JZ-GTE Engine

At the heart of every turbocharged Supra MK4 beats the immortal 2JZ-GTE inline-six engine. Frequently Asked Questions About Supra MK4

Q: What is the Supra MK4 0-60 time?

Stock Supra MK4 achieves 0-60 mph in 4.6 seconds with the 6-speed manual transmission. The automatic version is slightly slower at 4.9 seconds. This was exceptional performance for the 1990s and remains competitive today. Modified Supra MK4 with 1000+ HP can achieve sub-3 second 0-60 times, making them faster than most supercars. The quarter-mile time stock is 13.1-13.2 seconds at 109 mph.

Q: What is the Supra MK4 0-100 time? How fast is it?

Stock Supra MK4 0-100 mph time is approximately 10.2-10.5 seconds, making it one of the fastest cars of the 1990s. This was faster than contemporary Nissan 300ZX and Corvette models. The electronically limited top speed is 155 mph. For heavily modified Supra MK4 with 1000+ horsepower, the 0-100 time drops to around 6-7 seconds, comparable to modern hypercars. This demonstrates the exceptional tuning potential of the 2JZ-GTE engine.

Q: What mods should I do to my Supra MK4 first?

Best first modifications for Supra MK4 are: (1) ECU Tune - adds 50-80 HP instantly, cost-effective, ~₹30-50K; (2) Exhaust System - improves flow, adds 20-30 HP, better sound; (3) Intake/Air Filter - improves engine breathing; (4) Intercooler Upgrade - critical for reliability during tuning. These Stage 1 mods add ~100-150 HP with minimal risk. For serious builds, single turbo conversion and fuel system upgrades follow. A properly tuned stock 2JZ can reliably handle 400-450 HP before internal upgrades are needed.

Q: How much does a 1000 HP Supra MK4 cost in India?

Supra MK4 price for a 1000+ HP build in India: Base vehicle (₹25-50L) + modifications (₹15-25L) = Total ₹40-75 Lakhs. This includes: ECU tuning (₹30-50K), turbo upgrades (₹3-5L), fuel system (₹2-3L), internal engine modifications (₹5-8L), exhaust and cooling systems (₹2-3L). Compared to factory hypercars costing ₹1+ Crore, a 1000 HP Supra MK4 offers supercar performance at a fraction of the cost. Many enthusiasts build Stage 2 (600 HP) versions for ₹50-65L total investment.

Q: Can you make a stock Supra MK4 1200 HP reliably?

Yes, absolutely. The 2JZ-GTE is famous for handling 1200+ HP reliably. Thousands of Supra MK4s worldwide are running 1000+ HP builds with proven durability. The engine features: reinforced pistons rated for extreme pressure, forged steel crankshaft, robust cylinder head, and sequential twin-turbo system. At 1000+ HP, you need: (1) Internal engine rebuilds with quality components; (2) Fuel system capable of high flow; (3) Proper ECU tuning and boost control; (4) Regular maintenance and quality oil. Many tuners report 100k+ miles on 1000 HP Suprass with minimal issues. This makes the 2JZ-GTE one of the most reliable extreme performance engines ever built.

Q: Is Supra MK4 better than MK5? Which is faster?

Stock Performance: MK5 (382 HP) is faster than MK4 (320 HP) in standard form. Tuned Performance: MK4 wins decisively - 1200+ HP potential vs MK5 500 HP limit. Affordability: MK4 is significantly cheaper in India (₹25-150L) vs MK5 (import only, $45k+). Modification Community: MK4 has 30+ years of development vs MK5 which is newer. Complete Supra MK4 Engine Guide

The Toyota Supra MK4 (A80) features the legendary 2JZ-GTE twin-turbo engine delivering 321 horsepower and 315 lb-ft torque in stock form. This 3.0-liter displacement powerhouse accelerates 0-60 mph in 4.6 seconds with a top speed of 155 mph (electronically limited to 180 km/h). The Supra MK4 price in India ranges from ₹25-60 lakhs for quality stock examples, while modified Supra MK4 builds cost ₹60-150 lakhs depending on horsepower targets and modification stages.

The Supra MK4 is highly tuneable, capable of reaching 1200+ horsepower with proper ECU tuning, turbo upgrades, and internal engine modifications. Popular Supra MK4 modifications include single turbo conversions, intercooler upgrades, fuel system enhancements, manifold upgrades, and boost controller installations. Performance builds typically use stage 1 tuning (450 HP), stage 2 modifications (600-800 HP), and stage 3 extreme builds (1000+ HP). The 2JZ-GTE engine specifications include 8.5:1 compression ratio, sequential CT20 turbochargers at 11 PSI stock boost (upgradeable to 20+ PSI), Getrag V160 6-speed manual transmission, and dry-sump lubrication supporting extreme performance. Supra MK4 0-60 times improve dramatically with modifications: stage 2 builds achieve 3.5-4.0 seconds, while 1000+ HP Supra MK4 builds reach sub-3 second 0-60 acceleration matching supercar performance.

For Supra MK4 buyers in India, pricing varies by condition: second-hand Supra MK4 prices start at ₹25-40 lakhs for high-mileage examples, mid-range used Supra MK4s cost ₹45-80 lakhs for well-maintained examples, and low-mileage Supra MK4 on OLX fetch ₹85-150 lakhs.
