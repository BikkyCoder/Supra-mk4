Supra MK4 Pricing FAQs

Why is Supra MK4 price so high in India compared to USA?

Import duties, customs taxes, registration fees, and exchange rates add 40-50% to vehicle cost. Additionally, limited supply and high demand from collectors drives up Indian market prices.

What is a good price for a second-hand Supra MK4?

In USA: $40-60K for good condition. In India: â‚¹25-35 lakhs ($30-42K). Look for low mileage (below 100K miles), service history, and no major accident records.

How much to import a Supra MK4 from Japan to India?

Total cost approximately 40-50% above vehicle price. Includes customs duty (60%), basic customs duty (5%), GST (28%), port charges, shipping ($2-3K), and registration/clearance.

What impacts Supra MK4 resale value?

Mileage, maintenance history, accident history, modifications quality, original parts, color condition, interior condition, engine reliability, and market demand. Modified examples often retain value better with quality documentation.

Is Supra MK4 a good investment/collector car?

Yes! MK4 prices have appreciated 40-60% over past 5 years. Well-maintained examples hold value exceptionally well. Rare colors and low mileage examples command premium prices. Paul Walker association increases cultural value.

Where to find Supra MK4 for sale in India and Pakistan?

Online: OLX.in, CarDekho, Cars24. Auctions: BaT Auctions (international shipping available). Dealers: Contact classic car importers in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore. Direct imports: Contact JDM import specialists in Japan.
