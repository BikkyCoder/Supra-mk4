Importing Supra MK4 to India - Procedure & Costs

Import Process Steps

  1. Source the Car: Japan, USA, or Middle East dealers
  2. Arrange Shipping: Container or RoRo (Roll-on/Roll-off)
  3. Port Clearance: Customs duty, GST, documentation
  4. Vehicle Inspection: RTO inspection and certification
  5. Registration: Vehicle registration under Indian law
  6. Insurance: Vehicle insurance policy obtained
