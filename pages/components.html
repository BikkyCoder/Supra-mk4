Supra MK4 Components FAQ: Parts, Diagrams & Pricing

Common questions about JZA80 parts, MK4 vs MK5 components, carbon fiber upgrades, and availability

Complete JZA80 parts diagrams and comprehensive parts list PDFs are available online through EPC parts catalogs, Toyota technical documentation, and community resources. Download sections include engine diagram, suspension layout, interior parts breakdown, and electrical schematics for restoration and modification projects.

Popular carbon fiber parts for JZA80 MK4 include CF hood, CF spoiler, CF side skirts, CF dashboard kit, CF mirrors, and CF interior trim pieces. These upgrades combine weight reduction with performance improvements. Pricing varies by quality and supplier, with options available for USA, Australia, and international markets.

A80 vs A90 (MK5) Supra: A80 has 2JZ-GTE engine with 1000+ HP tuning potential vs A90 B58 turbo. A80 has more aftermarket parts/body kit options and better component availability globally. A80 interior parts more readily available. A90 has more OEM carbon fiber parts. A80 offers superior tuning flexibility; A90 more modern features but less customization history.

Top MK4 interior parts include OEM leather seats, dashboard assembly, door panels, carpet and trim kits, steering wheel, climate control, and dashboard covers. Restoration-grade parts available from specialty suppliers. Pricing varies by condition (OEM original vs reproduction). Availability good in USA, moderate in Australia, limited in India market.

Buy MK4 Supra parts from eBay, specialty auto retailers, JDM importers, local workshops, online catalogs (USA), and specialized suppliers in Australia. Limited direct availability in India; most imports from Japan/USA. Check part diagrams and specifications before purchase. Compare OEM vs aftermarket pricing across markets.

Popular MK4 headlight upgrades include LED retrofit kits, HID conversion kits, custom housings, and modern projector-style replacements. OEM parts provide OEM fitment; aftermarket options offer better visibility and modern aesthetics. Prices vary based on technology (LED vs HID). Installation difficulty ranges from moderate (DIY) to complex (custom housings).

Supra MK4 availability in India is very limited; few original units exist. Most parts must be imported from USA or Australia. JZA80 parts catalog access limited but growing. Carbon fiber parts, interior parts, and body kits available through import channels. Custom shop sourcing required. Prices significantly higher due to import duties and shipping costs.

Supra generations: MK1 (1978-1981), MK2 (1981-1986), MK3 (1986-1993), MK4/A80 (1993-2002 - 2JZ engine, most tunable), MK5/A90 (2019-present - B58 engine, modern tech). MK7 is unconfirmed (theoretical future). MK4 most popular for tuning; MK3 rare; MK5 newest but less customizable. Components completely different between generations.
