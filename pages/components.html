Core components that unleash the true potential of the legendary 2JZ-GTE engine. These performance-focused upgrades are essential for building a reliable high-horsepower Supra.

Turbo Exhaust Manifold

A high-performance exhaust manifold is crucial for maximizing turbo efficiency and power output. Equal-length runner design ensures optimal exhaust gas flow, minimizing turbo lag and maximizing response. Essential for builds targeting 500+ HP, with advanced thermal management for reliability.

Material 304 Stainless Steel
Design Equal Length Runners

Large Single Turbo Kit

Popular upgrade replacing the factory twin-turbo setup for dramatic horsepower gains. Offers better response and simplified plumbing compared to the stock sequential system.

Includes Full Kit + Hardware

Forged Internals Package

Essential upgrade for high-power builds, including forged pistons and connecting rods. Necessary for handling extreme boost levels and ensuring long-term reliability.
