MK4 Supra Release Date & History

Originally released in May 1993 in Japan and 1994 in global markets, the Toyota Supra MK4 (JZA80) continued production until 2002. The twin-turbo variant was available from launch until August 2002, making it one of the most iconic Japanese sports cars ever produced.

2JZ-GTE Engine Overview

The legendary 2JZ-GTE is a masterpiece of engineering, known for its incredible durability and tuning potential. The iron block construction and robust internals have made it one of the most respected engines in tuning culture.
