Supra MK4 Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the legendary 2JZ-GTE engine and MK4 specifications

What is the Supra MK4 horsepower and top speed?

The stock Toyota Supra MK4 produces 321 HP from its 2JZ-GTE twin-turbo engine with a top speed of 155 mph (electronically limited). With ECU remaps and turbo upgrades, tuned Supra MK4 models can easily exceed 1000+ HP with 0-60 times under 3 seconds.

How much is a Supra MK4 price in India and other markets?

Supra MK4 pricing varies by market and condition. In India, second-hand MK4 Supras typically range from $25,000-$150,000 depending on mileage and modifications. Used models are significantly cheaper than mint condition examples. Dubai market prices are similar, while USA prices fluctuate based on collector car demand.

What is the 2JZ-GTE engine and its tuning potential?

The 2JZ-GTE is a 3.0L inline-6 twin-turbocharged engine producing 321 HP stock with 315 lb-ft torque. Its robust design allows extreme tuning: single turbo conversions, ECU remaps, intercooler upgrades, and boost level increases enable builds reaching 500 HP, 800 HP, or even 1200+ HP reliably, making it one of the most tuneable engine platforms ever created.

What are the main differences between MK3, MK4, and MK5 Supra?

The MK3 Supra (1986-1992) featured a 7M-GTE engine, while the legendary MK4 (1993-2002) introduced the superior 2JZ-GTE. The newer MK5 (2019+) uses a BMW-sourced B58 turbocharged inline-6 but lacks the cultural impact and tuning legacy of the MK4. The MK4 remains the most sought-after for enthusiasts and collectors.

What is Paul Walker's Supra MK4 from Fast and Furious?

Paul Walker's iconic orange Supra MK4 (with license plate "JDMPOWER") from the Fast and Furious franchise became one of the most recognizable cars in cinema history. This cultural appearance dramatically increased MK4 demand among enthusiasts and new generations, establishing it as THE JDM legend and influencing modification trends worldwide.

What are the best Supra MK4 turbo kits and performance mods?

Popular Supra MK4 modifications include: HKS turbo kits, T67 single-turbo conversions, larger intercoolers, Garrett turbochargers, custom exhaust systems, ECU tuning via standalone units or piggyback controllers, suspension upgrades, and fuel system enhancements. Turbo kit installation typically boosts power to 500+ HP with significant reliability improvements.

How to find Supra MK4 for sale near Rajasthan or India?

Supra MK4 availability in India is limited due to import restrictions. Options include: classic car dealers specializing in imports, online marketplaces (OLX), international auctions (BaT Auctions), contacting JDM importers, or joining Supra MK4 community groups. Prices vary by condition, mileage, modifications, and whether it's a second-hand domestic import or new listing.

What is the Supra MK4 0-60 time and performance capabilities?

Stock Supra MK4 achieves 0-60 mph in 4.6 seconds with manual transmission. With turbo upgrades and ECU optimization, tuned examples easily drop to 3.5-4.0 seconds. Heavily modified drag-oriented builds (1000+ HP) achieve sub-3 second 0-60 times. Top speed is limited to 155 mph electronically, removable with tuning.

Are there Supra MK4 wallpaper and photo resources available?

Yes! High-quality Supra MK4 resources include: 4K wallpapers for phones/desktops, HD photos of stock and modified examples, technical drawings, CAD models, and visual galleries showcasing different color options (Black, Red, White, Silver, Blue). Many communities offer free downloads, while professional photographers sell premium collections.

Is the Supra MK4 better than Nissan GTR for performance?

Supra MK4 vs GTR comparison depends on generation. The modern R35 GTR significantly outperforms the MK4 in stock form, but tuned MK4 Supras with high boost levels can compete. However, MK4 enthusiasts value the analog driving experience, simpler mechanics, legendary 2JZ reliability, and cultural significance over raw performance metrics.
