1998 Toyota Supra MK4 FAQ - Price, Specs & Top Speed

Answers to frequently asked questions about Supra MK4 price in India, horsepower specs, mileage, top speed, MK5 comparison, and modified variants

What is the 1998 Toyota Supra MK4 horsepower and top speed?

The stock 1998 Toyota Supra MK4 produces 321 HP @ 5600 RPM from its 2JZ-GTE twin-turbo engine with 315 lb-ft torque. Top speed is 155 mph (250 km/h) electronically limited. With ECU remaps and turbo upgrades, modified Supra MK4 builds easily exceed 1000+ HP with 0-60 times under 3 seconds and top speeds exceeding 200 mph.

What is the Supra MK4 price in India 2025 - second hand and on-road?

Supra MK4 price in India varies significantly. Second-hand MK4 models typically range from ‚Çπ25-150 lakhs depending on mileage and condition. On-road price (with taxes and registration) ranges from ‚Çπ28-160 lakhs. In 2025, pristine examples command premium prices due to rarity, while modified variants may cost more. Prices fluctuate based on import availability and collector demand.

What is the Supra MK4 mileage and fuel efficiency?

The Toyota Supra MK4 achieves approximately 17-20 MPG in city driving and 22-24 MPG on highways, depending on driving habits and engine tuning. Stock MK4 models have better fuel economy, while heavily modified turbocharged builds sacrifice efficiency for performance. Fuel tank capacity is 71.6 liters (18.9 gallons), providing good range for spirited driving.

How does Toyota Supra MK4 compare to MK5 price, specs, and performance?

Supra MK5 price in India is significantly higher (‚Çπ1.5-2 crores for new imports) compared to second-hand MK4 options. MK5 produces 335 HP stock (less than tuned MK4) and features a BMW B58 engine with less tuning potential. MK4 advantages: legendary 2JZ-GTE (1200+ HP capable), iconic status, proven reliability. MK5 advantages: modern technology, automatic transmission, better warranty.

What is the Toyota Supra MK3 price in India and how does it compare to MK4?

Toyota Supra MK3 price in India is typically ‚Çπ15-60 lakhs (rare, older generation from 1986-1992). MK3 features a 7M-GTE engine producing only 300 HP - significantly less powerful than MK4's 321 HP stock, and far inferior to modified MK4 builds reaching 1000+ HP. MK4 top speed (155 mph) also exceeds MK3. The MK4 is superior in every performance metric and reliability.

How much horsepower can a modified Supra MK4 produce and what is the top speed?

Modified Supra MK4 horsepower ranges dramatically: Stage 1 tuning yields 450-500 HP, Stage 2 reaches 600-800 HP, and extreme builds with single turbo conversions exceed 1000-1200 HP reliably. Modified top speed exceeds 200 mph (with limiter removal). The 2JZ-GTE engine's robust design makes it legendary for extreme tuning potential - many examples worldwide run 1000+ HP successfully.

What is Paul Walker's Supra MK4 from Fast and Furious and its significance?

Paul Walker's iconic orange Supra MK4 (with "JDMPOWER" license plate) from Fast and Furious became cinema history's most recognizable sports car. This cultural phenomenon dramatically increased global MK4 demand, particularly in India. The film appearance established the Supra MK4 as THE JDM legend, influencing modification trends and making it highly sought-after by collectors and enthusiasts worldwide.

What are the best Supra MK4 turbo kits for performance enhancement and modified builds?

Top Supra MK4 turbo kits include: HKS turbo kits, T67 single-turbo conversions, Garrett turbochargers, larger intercoolers, and custom exhaust systems. Combined with ECU tuning (standalone or piggyback controllers) and fuel system upgrades, these modifications boost power to 500+ HP reliably. Stage 3 single turbo conversions enable 1000+ HP builds. The 2JZ-GTE engine handles extreme modifications exceptionally well.

How to find and purchase a Supra MK4 for sale in India with best price?

Finding Supra MK4 for sale in India requires multiple approaches: classic car dealers specializing in JDM imports, online marketplaces (OLX, BaT Auctions), contacting JDM importers directly, or joining Supra MK4 community groups and forums. Supra MK4 price in India varies by mileage, modification status, import source, and condition. Second-hand imports are most affordable; pristine examples command premium pricing.

What is the stock Supra MK4 0-60 time and how fast is the top speed?

Stock Toyota Supra MK4 achieves 0-60 mph in 4.6 seconds with manual transmission. The top speed is 155 mph (250 km/h) electronically limited. Quarter-mile time is 13.1 seconds @ 109 mph. With turbo upgrades and tuning, 0-60 times drop to 3.5-4.0 seconds for moderately tuned builds. Extreme 1000+ HP builds achieve sub-3 second 0-60 times. Top speed limiter removal enables 200+ mph capability.

Is the Supra MK4 better than Nissan GTR? Performance and reliability comparison?

Supra MK4 vs Nissan GTR performance depends on generation. Modern R35 GTR outperforms stock MK4 significantly. However, tuned 1000+ HP Supra MK4 builds can match GT-R acceleration. MK4 advantages: legendary 2JZ reliability, simpler mechanics, extreme tuning capability, iconic status, better customization. GTR advantages: AWD system, launch control, modern technology. Enthusiasts prefer MK4 for pure driving experience and modification potential.

Supra MK4 availability in India is extremely limited due to import restrictions. Estimates suggest only 50-200 units exist in India (exact numbers unavailable). What is the Toyota Supra MK4 top speed in km/h without electronic limiter?

Toyota Supra MK4 electronically limited top speed is 155 mph (250 km/h). With limiter removal, stock MK4 is estimated to reach 165-175 mph (265-280 km/h) depending on conditions. Modified builds with turbo upgrades and tuning can achieve 200+ mph (320+ km/h). However, actual testing at these speeds requires specialized racing facilities for safety. The 2JZ-GTE engine supports these velocities reliably.
